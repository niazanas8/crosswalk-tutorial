Modern detectors use deep networks:

  • Object Detection: YOLO / Faster R‑CNN variants trained on crosswalk boxes.
  • Semantic Segmentation: U‑Net / DeepLab to segment zebra stripes or crosswalk areas.
  • Efficiency on Edge: Model compression and Jetson‑class deployment for real‑time inference [7].

Advantages include better robustness to wear/occlusion; limitations include dataset bias and performance at night or in extreme weather.
