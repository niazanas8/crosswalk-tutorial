Challenges in Crosswalk Detection

Appearance Variability

Different countries and cities use distinct markings (zebra, ladder, continental). Paint wear and resurfacing reduce contrast.

Occlusion

Vehicles or crowds may partially cover markings, lowering detector confidence and increasing false negatives.

Illumination & Weather

Harsh shadows, nighttime, glare, rain/snow introduce noise; HDR ranges can saturate.

Viewpoint & Scale

Onboard cameras view crosswalks at angles and varying distances; perspective distortion changes stripe geometry.

Domain Shift

Models trained in one city may not generalize to another due to markings, pavement color, or camera intrinsics.
